About me

Myself Tejas Nyalpelly, I have completed my graduation in Computer Engineering in 2014 from Mumbai University. Later I did a Diploma course for a year in Vfx and Animation from MAAC academy. During the course I got an offer from Prana Studio as CHF artist. There I worked in projects like Angela's Christmas and Watership down. Then after a year I joined Resonance Digital where I was working as an Animator for various Commercial ads and even Vfx projects.

Due to lack of job satisfaction I left that company and worked on my reel which was based on creature so I needed good time.After completion I got an oppurunity from Technicolor for an animation training which was for 2 months. After completing my certified training I started doing freelancing. I am very much interested in Acting and Creature animation so made a youtube channel of my own named TVN videos where I have uploaded my own animated videos and will be uploading more.
